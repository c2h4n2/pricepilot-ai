How PricePilot Scores Products

Our mission is simple: Help you find the right tech product without spending hours comparing specifications. Every recommendation on PricePilot AI follows the same evaluation process.

Our 100-Point Value Score

Every product receives a Value Score out of 100. The score is based on several important factors—not just price.

  • Performance
  • Features
  • Build quality
  • User ratings
  • Price compared with similar products
  • Best use case
  • Overall value for money

A higher score means the product delivers stronger overall value—not simply that it is the most expensive.

How Our AI Recommendations Work

When you use the AI Shopping Assistant, PricePilot looks at:

  • Your budget
  • The type of product you're looking for
  • How you plan to use it
  • Important features you mention
  • The overall value score of each product

Our AI recommends products that best match your needs instead of simply suggesting the most expensive option.

Our Principles

  • Independent — Products are evaluated using the same scoring criteria.
  • Transparent — We explain why products are recommended.
  • Value First — Higher prices do not automatically receive higher scores.
  • User Focused — Recommendations are designed around real buying scenarios.
